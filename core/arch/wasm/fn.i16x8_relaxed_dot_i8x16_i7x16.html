A relaxed dot-product instruction.

This instruction will perform pairwise products of the 8-bit values in a and b and then accumulate adjacent pairs into 16-bit results producing a final i16x8 vector. The bytes of a are always interpreted as signed and the bytes in b may be interpreted as signed or unsigned.
